Court In Use (PROD Version)

Simple sign-in for community tennis courts

{/* Feature cards */}

✓ Not a Reservation System

No accounts or logins. Players simply tap Click to Start on their phone at the court to record the start time.”

✓ Enforce Time-Limit Rules

Track when play started on each court so rules like “Limit total playing time to 45 mins when others are waiting” can be applied fairly.

✓ Check From Home

Players can check how busy the courts are and decide whether to go.

✓ Help Administrators

Court managers can understand real usage patterns without installing expensive access systems.

✓ For Community Tennis Courts

Ideal for community with 1-6 tennis courts. Just post a QR code on the courts.
